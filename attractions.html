Attractions

Find fun things to do during your trip here! All of these attractions can be found on the maps page!

Lincoln Memorial

A 19-ft. tall marble monument of the 16th President of the United States, Abraham Lincoln.

Washington Monument

A towering obelisk honors the United States's first President, George Washington. It opens at 9am and closes at 10pm everyday of the week.

White House

Residence and workplace of the President of the United States.

United States Capitol

Home of the United States Congress. Located on Capitol Hill.

Smithsonian Institution

Admission free complex. Contains 19 museums, a zoo, and nine research centers.

Thomas Jefferson Memorial

Memorial dedicated to Thomas Jefferson, the writer of the Declaration of Independence.

Arlington National Cemetary

A very large military cemetary. From the Civil War onward, many of the nation's fallen have been buried here.

National Air and Space Museum

This museum documents the history of space exploration and aviation. It has several vehicles on display.
