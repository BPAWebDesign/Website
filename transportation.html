Transportation Methods

These are some of the ways you can get around Washington D.C.!

Walking

Walking is free and Washington D.C. is a very walkable city as it has a lot of sidewalks.

Metro

The Metro has 91 stations and 6 different lines that go across D.C. During the week, the Metro opens at 5am and at 7am on the weekends. It runs until 3am on Fridays and Saturdays. On all other days it runs until midnight. Eating and drinking is not allowed on the Metro. This is one of the reasons why the Metro in the city is so clean.

Renting a Bike

Renting a bike in Washington D.C. is very easy by using Capital Bikeshare. The company allows you to rent a bike for a low cost. Their options for renting are very flexible for wherever you want to go to. They charge $2.00 foor 30 minutes and $8 for a day of riding. You can check the availability of the bikes on their website under the maps tab.

Uber or Lyft

Using one of these companies, you can get yourself a ride to wherever you want in D.C. It is very easy to order a Uber or Lyft using a smartphone. They are more expensive compared to other transportation options, but the convinience of using their services make it worth it.
