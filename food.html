Food and Restaurants

BLT Prime by David Burke

This luxorious restaurant specializes in top-of-the-line steaks and popovers. The most expensive of the three, located in the Trump International Hotel.

Address: 1100 Pennsylvania Ave NW, Washington, DC 20004

The Hamilton

This American restaurant serves what you would expect: burgers, steaks, and more. It is not too expensive and is a great option for a good meal.

Address: 600 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005

Timber Pizza Company

This pizza place is good for quick bite to eat. They have great oven-fire pizzas. The least expensive option, but still having good food.

Address: 809 Upshur St NW, Washington, DC 20011
