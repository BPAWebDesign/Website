Clothing and Dress Code

Know what and what not to wear to the BPA NLC Competition

Women:

This list contains the proper attire for Women attending the BPA NLC.

  • Dress skirt or dress slacks
  • Blazers
  • Dress shoes or sandals with heel are required
  • Length of skirt can be no shorter than 2 inches above the kneecap

Men:

This list contains the proper attire for Men attending the BPA NLC.

  • Suit or sport coat (optional)
  • Blazer with tie and dress shirt
  • Dress shoes and dress socks are required
  • Dress slacks

Unacceptable Clothing:

Do NOT wear these clothing items to the BPA NLC.

  • Ragged or cutoff clothing
  • Bathing suits
  • Clothing that contains inappropriate images or words
  • Strapless shirts, midriff tops, halter tops, spaghetti straps, and tube tops
  • Spandex
  • Transparent clothing
